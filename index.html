Trip Overview

Hello and welcome to my page! Below, I have visually and descriptively elaborated on the highlights from my solo trip to Indonesia in 2015. Over the course of 10 weeks, I travelled throughout the islands of Bali, the Gilis, Komodo, Flores, Sulawesi, Irian Jaya (West Papua). From exotic wildlife to former cannibals, I experienced one of the greatest trips in my life.
